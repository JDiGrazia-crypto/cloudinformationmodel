Shipment

Tracking the physical packages actually shipped for an order.

Entity Groups

Name Description
Shipment Example Entities included: Shipment, Shipment Package, Shipment Document, Shipment Product

Entities

Name Description
Shipment A quantity of goods sent together to a place, or the act of sending them
Shipment Document A receipt, bill of lading or other document that is delivered with the shipment to explain the purchase or shipment contents.
Shipment Package A parcel of one or more items bundled together, so that they arrive at the customer's address as a single unit.
Shipment Product An item on a list of one or more that comprises a shipment.
Shipment Product Price Adjustment A change to the price of an ordered product or service that is made after Fulfillment.
Shipment Product Price Adjustment Tax A tax on the price of a Sales Order Product.
Shipment Status The stage of a shipment. Possible values could be pre-transit, in transit, delayed, delivered
Shipping Method A parcel of one or more items bundled together, so that they arrive at the customer's address as a single unit.
